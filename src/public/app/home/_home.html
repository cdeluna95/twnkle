Your Astrology Sign, Matched Up

Twnkle is an astrology-based social dating app helping to find your match based on your sign. This app tries to solve a societal problem of judging a book by its cover, as we emphasize your zodiac signs and personal characteristics when finding and matching with other people.

Find

Sign up and enter your date of birth, sign, interests, and profile picture! Once you're done, find who you're matched with and talk to them!

Chat

Our app has a built-in chat system along with all of your potential matches and contacts you've followed or friended. Hit them up and set up a time to meet up!

Meet

Successful? Hang out with them, have a meaningful conversation, and have fun! Tell your friends to join in and ask them what their sign is!
